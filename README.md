# Compass

**Describe your situation in plain language. Get a clear, ready-to-file path to local aid.**

Compass is an agentic web assistant that turns a messy, real-life crisis description into a personalized, ready-to-act packet of local assistance programs — the right offices, forms, and documents — grounded in a real local resource directory, with a human in control of every irreversible step.

> Compass prepares; **you decide and file**. It is not an official benefits determination and never submits anything to an agency on your behalf.

## How it works

You describe your situation, typed or spoken. An LLM-driven agent interprets it, plans multi-step lookups against a real Hudson/Bergen resource directory, checks rough eligibility through a short guided Q&A, and assembles a grounded action plan: the best-matched programs, why each matched, required documents, locations, hours, and a drafted cover email. Nothing is sent until you approve.

## Responsible AI

Every match shows its eligibility reasoning and a confidence level. Low-confidence cases, anything involving a minor's benefits, and cross-agency conflicts are flagged for a human. The system has no autonomous submit function, and a crisis hotline surfaces immediately for emergencies.

## Disclaimer

Compass provides informational guidance only. It does not make official eligibility determinations and does not submit applications. Always confirm with the relevant office. In an emergency, contact your local crisis hotline or emergency services.
